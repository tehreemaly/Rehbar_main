QUICK REFERENCE — MOST USED CONTACTS


SECTION 1 — FEDERAL EDUCATION BODIES

These are the main national organizations for higher education admissions, degree verification, scholarships, and federal school policies.

SECTION 2 — PROVINCIAL EDUCATION DEPARTMENTS & EXAM BOARDS

Each province manages its own school system, college admissions, and intermediate/secondary board exams. Contact your provincial BISE for result, rechecking, or certificate issues.

SECTION 3 — SCHOLARSHIPS & FREE TRAINING PROGRAMS

Pakistan offers multiple government-funded scholarships. HEC Benazir Undergraduate Scholarship is the most accessible for low-income students. NAVTTC provides free vocational training.

SECTION 4 — HOW TO APPLY FOR HEC BENAZIR SCHOLARSHIP (BUS)

Step 1: Visit hec.gov.pk/scholarships during the announcement window (usually August-October). Download the BUS application form.
Step 2: Eligibility check: Family income must be under Rs. 45,000/month. You must be enrolled in a public HEC-recognized university with minimum 2.0 CGPA.
Step 3: Prepare documents: CNIC of parent/guardian, income certificate from Union Council/Patwari, university enrollment letter, latest transcript.
Step 4: Submit application at your university's Financial Aid Office (FAO). The FAO forwards it to HEC. Do NOT send directly to HEC by post.
Step 5: Check status at hec.gov.pk student portal or call 051-111-119-432. Disbursement: tuition paid directly to university + Rs. 4,000/month stipend to your bank account.
